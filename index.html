Introduction

What is beauty? What if you could test for beauty like we test for Math or English? Our project seeks to identify what the American beauty standard is and map out the mathematical relationships that define it. However, we believe that beauty is much more than your face shape, some mathematically defined score, or some photoshopped model in a magazine; therefore, this project will be about taking those beauty standards and completely rejecting them. This project will be about denying the media’s defined “beauty” and embracing individuality and inner beauty by exemplifying the unique characteristics that make people who they are. This is for all of the people out there who struggle with body image and for a push to change how our society views beauty from a strict model-esque image to something much more diverse and realistic. If this project can make just one person rethink their definition of beauty or see one new person in a different, more positive light than before then this project will be a complete success in our minds.
